COMPANY OVERVIEW

Synthemica (Pvt) Ltd, Synthemica Private Limited is a globally recognized organization specializing in the sourcing of high-quality raw materials and advanced manufacturing solutions.

As a proud member of its parent group, Synthemica is committed to maintaining international quality standards, ensuring excellence, reliability, and compliance across all its manufacturing and supply operations.

DIRECTOR BOARD

Mr. Senitha Wanigasinghe

Mr. Senitha Wanigasinghe

Managing Director - Synthemica Private Limited

Mr. Senitha Wanigasinghe is a Chartered Chemist and graduated from Imperial College London in the United Kingdom with M.Sci honours in Medicinal chemistry. He has previously worked in numerous international organizations such as Livisto, Spain and GlaxoSmithKline, UK.

He has gained experience in the pharmaceutical and consumer health care industry specifically in the Research & development of new products and analytical chemistry.

 

Dr. Thulitha Wanigasinghe

Dr. Thulitha Wanigasinghe

Managing Director - Synthemica Private Limited

Dr. Thulitha Wanigasinghe holds a Bachelor of Veterinary Medicine from Royal Veterinary College, United Kingdom. He currently holds memberships in Veterinary boards of United Kingdom, Sri Lanka and Australia. Collecting wealth of knowledge from different countries he has completed a one year Veterinary Surgical Internship in Australia.

Alongside working in diverse environments’, he plans to implement and develop Sri Lankan Animal Health Industry to next level.
