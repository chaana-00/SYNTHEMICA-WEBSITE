Mr. Chanaka Indrajith

Mr. Chanaka Indrajith

Director/COO - Medivesta Private Limited

Mr. Chanaka Indrajith currently serves as the Director/Chief Operating Officer (COO) at Medivesta (Pvt) Limited within the Divesta Group of Companies.. A dedicated professional, he has played pivotal roles in pharmaceutical manufacturing management, quality assurance, and research and development since 2013.

He holds a Bachelor’s degree in Pharmacy from the University of Peradeniya and a Master of Business Administration (MBA) from the University of Wales, Post graduate diplomas in Medicinal chemistry, Business, Finance and Strategy. Mr. Indrajith is currently pursuing his Doctorate at the University of Colombo.

 

As a visiting lecturer at esteemed institutions such as the University of Peradeniya and the University of Ruhuna since 2012, he imparts his extensive knowledge in pharmaceutical Technology, management, quality assurance, and research and development.
